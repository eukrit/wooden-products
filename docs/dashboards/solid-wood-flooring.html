Scope & method

Included — genuine wood flooring only: (a) one-piece solid timber (solid, Firestore category timber_flooring), and (b) real-wood engineered / parquet (engineered, engineered_flooring) — multi-layer with a genuine solid-wood wear layer. Scope is decided per-record, not by category name alone: a flooring record is dropped if its material / subcategory / name matches a composite marker (WPC, SPC, LVT, vinyl, laminate, HDF/MDF/fiberboard, wood-/stone-plastic or -polymer composite).

Excluded entirely — WPC / artificial-wood / composite, SPC / stone-plastic, LVT / vinyl, and laminate / HDF / MDF "decor" floors. The Slack price list's pages 10–11 (金刚面 "diamond-face" & 强化 "reinforced", both "surface = HD decorative paper") are laminate and were left out.

Pricing — raw unit price taken from structured quotations.items[] (by product_id) first, else parsed from free-text notes; normalised to ≈THB/m² using a static, approximate FX table (THB per unit, as of 2026-06-10): THB≈1.0, USD≈36.5, CNY≈5.0, EUR≈40.0. Per-piece / per-linear-metre prices are converted to m² via product dimensions where possible; otherwise ≈THB/m² shows n/a. FX is hand-set, not a live feed. Prices are only ever shown when sourced.
