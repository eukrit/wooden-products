## Product Data — WPC Fence Systems

### Material: Wood Plastic Composite (WPC)

**What is WPC?**
- Engineered composite of wood fiber (60%) and polymer (40%)
- Looks and feels like natural wood but requires zero maintenance
- Resistant to rot, termites, UV fading, moisture, and warping
- Eco-friendly: made from recycled wood fiber and plastics
- Lifespan: 25+ years outdoors

### Two Product Lines

#### 1. Co-Extrusion Series (Premium)
- Shield-wrapped surface layer bonded over WPC core
- Superior UV resistance, scratch resistance, fade resistance
- Premium wood grain texture
- Available finishes: brushed, embossed, knife-cut texture
- Best for: hotels, resorts, high-end residential, commercial projects

#### 2. Standard WPC Series (Value)
- Traditional WPC construction
- 2D wood grain or 3D embossed surface options
- Excellent value for mid-range projects
- Best for: residential fencing, garden walls, commercial perimeters
