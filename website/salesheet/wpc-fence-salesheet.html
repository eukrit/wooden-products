Product Sales Sheet — 2026 Edition
Engineered Outdoor Fencing

Natural wood, reinvented for a maintenance-free life.

Premium wood-composite fence systems engineered for Thailand's climate.

The Leka Studio WPC Fence Collection is a complete, factory-engineered fencing system crafted from high-density wood-plastic composite. Each board combines 60% recycled wood fibre with 40% polymer binder, producing the warmth and texture of natural timber without the rot, termites, or annual upkeep. The Collection is delivered as a full kit — boards, aluminium posts, hardware, and gates — specified for residential, hospitality, and commercial projects.

Why Wood-Plastic Composite
Termite & Rot Proof
Closed-cell composite structure is impermeable to insects and fungal decay — the two principal causes of timber fence failure.
Dimensionally Stable
Low thermal movement and zero warp, cup, or split. Boards remain flat and aligned across seasons.
UV & Colour Stable
Co-extruded shield layer on Premium series delivers a decade of colour retention under direct sun exposure.
Recyclable & Low-Carbon
Diverts wood waste and plastic from landfill. Fully recyclable at end of service life.

Product Range

The Collection
Premium Series

Premium Co-Extrusion Fence Board

Shield-wrapped Composite

A shield-wrapped board engineered with a dense, fused surface layer bonded to the WPC core during extrusion. Delivers superior UV stability, scratch resistance, and a deep, embossed woodgrain texture. Recommended for hospitality, high-end residential, and signature commercial perimeters.

Classic Series

Classic WPC Fence Board

Solid Wood-Composite

A traditional wood-composite board with a natural grain or 3D embossed finish. Shares the termite- and rot-proof benefits of the Premium series in a streamlined specification. Recommended for residential boundaries, garden privacy, and commercial mid-scale projects.

Material Composition
Core — Wood-Plastic Composite
~60% recycled hardwood fibre bonded with ~40% food-grade HDPE polymer and UV-stabilising additives. Hollow-section extrusion for strength-to-weight efficiency.
Shield — ASA Surface (Premium only)
A thin, high-density ASA (acrylic-styrene-acrylonitrile) skin co-extruded onto all exposed faces. Seals the core, locks in pigment, and resists abrasion, staining and weathering.
