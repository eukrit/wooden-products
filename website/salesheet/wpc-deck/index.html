HDPE-polymer matrix — no rot, no absorption

The Leka Studio WPC Deck Collection is a complete outdoor-flooring system built on a 100% waterproof HDPE-polymer core bonded with recycled wood fibre. Every board is reversible — two finished faces, one SKU — and locks to the substructure with a hidden stainless-steel clip, so a professional crew can lay a 40 m² terrace in a day with no face-fixings visible.

The Collection

Two board widths, one reversible format

Wide-format Premium boards for open terraces and hospitality decks, and narrow-format Classic boards for tighter residential spans and feature borders — both reversible, both clip-locked, both on the same waterproof core.

Material Science

Built for the wet side

W
Waterproof by Design

The HDPE-polymer core is naturally hydrophobic — water absorption under 0.5% by weight. No swelling, no rot, no fungal growth after submersion, splash, or monsoon standing water. Ideal for pool surrounds and wet-area terraces.

R
Reversible Face

Two finished surfaces per board — brushed wood-grain on one side, fine groove on the reverse. Flip in place to refresh a worn zone, or specify mixed-face layouts for pattern work. One SKU, two looks, zero waste.

P
Pest- and Rot-Proof

The composite matrix offers no cellulose food source that is accessible to termites, weevils, or marine borers. No pressure treatment, no borate dip, no annual anti-rot sealing required.

E
Recycled Content

Every linear metre diverts wood saw-dust from furniture-factory waste streams and reclaimed HDPE from post-consumer packaging. Fully recyclable at end of service — no pressure-treatment chemistry, no toxic leachate, no burn emissions.

Installation

Grid below, clips between

A disciplined sub-deck saves decades of snagging. A certified Leka crew can frame and board a 40 m² residential terrace in a single day; we supply drawings and torque specs for in-house teams.

Typical assembly — aluminium joist, stainless clip, reversible WPC board
1
Prepare Substrate

Level the concrete slab, waterproof membrane, or pedestal grid. Fall 1 – 2% away from building for drainage.

2
Lay Joist Grid

Fix aluminium joists on 300 – 400 mm centres (≤ 350 mm for 97 mm Classic boards). Perimeter joist flush with deck edge.

3
Clip-Lock Boards

Starter clip, then slide each board into the next clip — no face-fixings visible. Maintain a 5 mm gap at every board joint and at perimeter trim.

4
Edge & Trim

Face-screw the final board through the aluminium L- or U-profile. Finish end-grain with matched trim cap or 45° mitre.

Limited Warranty

Backed for the long term, defined in writing

The Leka WPC Deck Collection is distributed by Leka Studio Co., Ltd. and carries a manufacturer-issued limited warranty. Warranty terms summarised below are indicative — the full written warranty document prevails and is delivered with every project.

Colour retention
Fade is measured, not assumed

Outdoor deck boards will shift in tone over time — particularly in the first 90 days of UV exposure, when surface pigment stabilises. Our warranty is written against a measurable industry standard (ΔE colour difference, ISO 4892-2 accelerated weathering) rather than against any subjective expectation of "no change":

  • Premium LKD-P-140 — colour shift shall not exceed ΔE ≤ 5 over 5 years of normal outdoor exposure with the specified anti-UV top coat applied.
  • Classic LKD-C-097 — colour shift shall not exceed ΔE ≤ 8 over 3 years of normal outdoor exposure with the specified anti-UV top coat applied.

ΔE < 5 is considered a visually acceptable change for outdoor building products. Uneven fade limited to areas in permanent shade, under planters, or in standing water, and shade variation between production lots, are not warrantable defects. Boards installed without the specified anti-UV coating carry a reduced surface warranty — confirm scope at order.

What is excluded
Claim conditions

Warranty is void or reduced where boards have been:

  • Installed by a non-certified team or in deviation from the Leka installation manual (joist spacing, board gap, clip torque).
  • Painted, stained, sealed, waxed, sanded, or modified after installation.
  • Submerged continuously, exposed to sea-water spray, or to pool-chemical splashback above normal use concentrations.
  • Damaged by impact, fire, vandalism, rolling loads above 250 kg/m², vehicle contact, or construction-site mishandling.
  • Installed within 600 mm of a heat source above 75 °C (grills, fire pits, exhaust vents).
  • Cleaned with solvent, chlorine bleach, acid cleaners, or pressure-washed above 1500 psi at close range.
  • Used as a structural floor element, ramp, or cantilever beyond the published joist-spacing and load tables.

Minor colour or grain variation between production batches is a characteristic of extruded wood-composite products and is not a warrantable defect. Physical samples should be confirmed against the production lot before acceptance.

Claim process
Written, within 60 days of defect

Claims must be submitted in writing to warranty@leka.studio within 60 days of the defect becoming apparent, with the original project reference, photographs, installation date and installer identity. Leka will inspect on-site if required and forward validated claims to the manufacturer for adjudication. Remedy is limited, at the manufacturer's discretion, to board replacement or material credit — the warranty does not cover labour, site access, removal or reinstallation, or any consequential, incidental, or indirect damages.
