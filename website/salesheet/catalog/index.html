Signature Series

Premium shielded profiles with a fused polymer cap layer protecting the WPC core from UV, stains, mould and scratching. Engineered for hospitality, high-spec residential and exposed façades.

Surface Finishes

Four engineered surface textures.

Brushed

Lightly wire-brushed surface that lifts the natural wood flour grain. Low-glare, soft-touch, understated. Ideal for hospitality decking and lift lobbies.

3D Embossed

Deep mechanically-pressed wood grain — the most convincing timber replica. Matches oak and walnut visual character. Hides minor scuffs naturally.

Knife-Cut

Rough-sawn linear texture mimicking hand-cut reclaimed timber. Strong directional shadows, high tactile character. Used on feature cladding walls.

Stipple

Fine random texture with minimal directional pattern. Anti-slip underfoot on decks; preferred for poolside and wet-area specifications.
