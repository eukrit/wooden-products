Wood fibre and post-consumer polymer

The Leka Studio WPC Fence Collection is a complete, factory-engineered fencing system crafted from high-density wood-plastic composite. Each board combines 60% recycled wood fibre with 40% polymer binder, producing the warmth and texture of natural timber without the rot, termites, or annual upkeep.

The Collection

Two series, one standard of craft

Premium co-extrusion for signature commercial and hospitality projects, and Classic WPC for residential and mid-scale commercial work — both with the same termite-proof, rot-proof composite core.

Premium Series
Shield-wrapped Composite

Premium Co-Extrusion Board

A shield-wrapped board with a dense, fused surface layer bonded to the WPC core during extrusion. Superior UV stability, scratch resistance, and a deep embossed woodgrain. Recommended for hotels, resorts, high-end residential, and signature commercial perimeters.

  • Thickness20 mm
  • Effective Width148 mm
  • UV Warranty10 years
  • Structural Warranty25 years
Classic Series
Solid Wood-Composite

Classic WPC Board

A traditional wood-composite board with natural grain or 3D embossed finish. Shares the termite- and rot-proof benefits of Premium in a streamlined specification. Recommended for residential boundaries, garden privacy, and mid-scale commercial projects.

  • Thickness20 – 26 mm
  • Effective Width148 mm
  • UV Warranty5 years

Material Science

Built for longevity

C
Core — Wood-Plastic Composite

Approximately 60% recycled hardwood fibre bonded with 40% food-grade HDPE polymer and UV-stabilising additives. Hollow-section extrusion gives an efficient strength-to-weight ratio and clean cut edges.

S
Shield — ASA Surface (Premium)

A thin, high-density ASA (acrylic-styrene-acrylonitrile) skin co-extruded onto all exposed faces. Seals the core, locks in pigment, and resists abrasion, staining, and long-term weathering.

F
Fire Performance

Both series carry a B1 self-extinguishing classification — suitable for boundary fencing in most residential and commercial contexts. Documentation available on request.

E
Environmental

Every linear metre diverts approximately 3 kg of wood waste and 2 kg of plastic from landfill. Fully recyclable at end of service life — no pressure treatment, no toxic leachate.

Installation

Factory-clipped, site-assembled

A clip-and-channel system lets trained installers complete a 20 m residential fence run in a single day. Certified installation teams are available nationwide through our project desk.

Typical assembly — aluminium frame, WPC panel infill
1
Set Posts

Anchor 80 × 80 mm aluminium posts to a concrete pad, cast-in footing, or pedestal with anchor bolts.

2
Fix Lower Rail

Attach the lower aluminium cover strip between posts, levelling against the finished ground line.

3
Slide Boards

Drop WPC boards into the post channel using the clip system — no face-fixing, no visible screws on the board face.

4
Cap & Close

Install the upper aluminium clamp strip and decorative post caps. Bay is complete and load-ready.
