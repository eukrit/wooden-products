Premium Co-Extrusion Fence Board

Shield-wrapped composite technology

Our premium co-extrusion fence boards feature an advanced polymer shield layer bonded over a high-density WPC core. This dual-layer construction delivers superior UV resistance, scratch resistance, and fade protection that outperforms traditional composites. The co-extruded surface maintains its rich wood-grain appearance for over a decade without staining, painting, or sealing. Available in brushed, embossed, and knife-cut textures for a truly natural wood aesthetic.

Best suited for hotels, resorts, high-end residential estates, and commercial projects where long-term appearance matters.

  • Width148mm
  • Thickness20mm
  • Lengths1.5m / 1.8m / 2.0m / 2.9m
  • Weight~2.2 kg/m
  • UV ResistanceExcellent (10+ years)
  • Fire RatingB1 (self-extinguishing)
  • Warranty25 years structural

Classic WPC Fence Board

Reliable composite performance

The Classic WPC fence board offers excellent value with proven wood-plastic composite construction. Available in 2D wood grain and 3D embossed surface options, these boards deliver the natural wood look with zero maintenance. Engineered from 60% recycled wood fiber and 40% polymer, they resist rot, termites, moisture, and warping throughout their 15-year warranty period.

Ideal for residential garden fencing, boundary walls, pool enclosures, and commercial perimeters where durability and value matter.

Aluminium Post System

Engineered aluminium alloy framework

Our fence post system is built from hollow aluminium alloy sections, delivering exceptional strength with minimal weight. Each post is precision-extruded at 80 x 80mm and finished with a durable powder-coated black surface that resists chipping, peeling, and corrosion in tropical climates. The modular design accommodates fence heights from 2.0m to 3.0m with adjustable bay widths from 1.5m to 2.9m.

Posts integrate seamlessly with both Co-Extrusion and Standard WPC boards via our aluminium clamp strip system. Custom powder-coat colors are available on request.

  • MaterialAluminium alloy hollow section
  • Post Size80 x 80mm
  • Heights Available2.0m / 2.5m / 3.0m
  • FinishPowder-coated black
  • Max Fence Height3.0m
  • Post Spacing1.5m - 2.9m (standard: 2.0m)
  • Custom ColorsAvailable on request
