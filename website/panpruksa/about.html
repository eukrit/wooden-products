Our Story

About Panpruksa

Heritage

A Century of
Wood Excellence

Panpruksa channels a legacy of Italian wood craftsmanship stretching back to 1902, now reimagined for Southeast Asian living.

Operated by Nubo International from Singapore, Panpruksa partners with the world's finest wood flooring manufacturers to bring premium hardwood surfaces to the region. Our products combine European engineering precision with an understanding of tropical climates and contemporary Asian design.

Philosophy

Aesthetics & Ethics

We believe in the synthesis between outer beauty and substance, appearance and essence. Our work harmonizes technology with nature, industry with the environment, performance with health.

Stability

Engineered multilayer construction resists warping and expansion, even in high-humidity tropical environments.

Precision

Micrometric joints and transversal incisions ensure each board fits perfectly, maintaining beauty over decades.

Beauty

Natural wood grain, hand-selected species, and artisanal finishes create surfaces that are works of art.

Long Life

Marine-grade bonding and premium birch support deliver flooring that lasts generations, not just years.

Respect for Nature

Certified raw materials from sustainably managed forests. Every tree harvested is replenished.

Innovation

From anti-bacterial Crystalcare finishes to magnetic installation systems, we push what wood can do.

Engineering

Multilayer
Technology

The technological heart of our flooring lies in the special multilayer birch support. Five or seven cross-grain layers bonded with marine-grade phenolic glue create a foundation of unmatched stability.

Transversal incisions and micrometric joints keep the hardwood floor stable over time, preventing warping even under the most demanding conditions of Southeast Asian climates.

Our Journey

Heritage Timeline

1902

The Beginnings

The Margaritelli family begins specializing in mechanical tools for agriculture and forestry in Umbria, Italy.

1984

The Invention

Prof. Guglielmo Giordano patents a revolutionary multilayer hardwood flooring technology, inaugurating a new era in wood surfaces.

2006

Design Meets Wood

Natural Genius launches at the Guggenheim Museum NYC, uniting world-renowned designers with hardwood flooring.

2024

Nubo International

Nubo International Pte. Ltd. is established in Singapore to bring premium European wood flooring to Southeast Asia.

2026

Panpruksa Launches

Panpruksa debuts as the region's destination for luxury hardwood flooring, serving Singapore, Thailand, and beyond.

Sustainability

The Original Green

Our commitment to sustainability is not a marketing claim — it is foundational. Every wood species we use comes from certified, sustainably managed forests where harvesting is balanced with regeneration.

Our multilayer technology itself is an act of environmental responsibility: by using a thin top layer of precious hardwood on a birch plywood base, we produce more flooring from fewer trees while delivering superior performance.
