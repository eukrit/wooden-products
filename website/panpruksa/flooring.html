Wood Flooring

Natural Beauty,
Engineered Precision

Premium multilayer hardwood flooring designed for the demands of tropical living.

Our engineered flooring combines the warmth and beauty of real hardwood with advanced multilayer construction that resists warping, cupping, and expansion — even in Southeast Asia's high humidity. Available in Oak, Teak, and Cherry with multiple finish and installation options.

Technology

Multilayer Construction

Every board features a precision-engineered multilayer core — real hardwood veneer bonded to cross-grain birch plywood using marine-grade adhesive. This construction delivers stability that solid wood simply cannot match in tropical climates.

Climate Resistant

Cross-grain plywood core prevents warping and cupping even at 80%+ humidity levels common in Southeast Asia.

Real Hardwood Surface

2-3mm genuine hardwood veneer delivers authentic grain, color, and feel that no laminate or vinyl can replicate.

Click-Lock Installation

Precision-milled locking profiles enable fast, glue-free floating installation over most existing subfloors.
