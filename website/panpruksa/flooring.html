Technical

Specifications

Finishes

Varnish

Naturplus2

Solvent-free varnish coating which emphasizes the wood's grain and authenticity. Features anti-bacterial Crystalcare technology.

Matte

Mattplus

Solvent-free varnish coating similar to an untreated surface in appearance and colour. Anti-bacterial Crystalcare technology.

Oil

Oleonature

Natural oil-based, air-dried finish with special protective treatment. High protection with convenient maintenance. Crystalcare technology.

Grades

Refined

Elegant

Mixed grain with colour variations and small knots. Extra wide boards (140mm+) may feature filled or partially filled knots.

Character

Charme

Mixed grain and colour variations. Widespread presence of knots, including filled or partially filled ones.

Rustic

Esprit

Mixed grain, naturally uneven hue variations, widespread presence of filled or open knots, encased, tight or only partially filled.

Technology

Multilayer Construction

Birch Plywood Core

5 or 7 cross-grain layers of North-European birch provide maximum stability and resist warping in tropical humidity.

Marine-Grade Glue

Phenolic adhesive between layers ensures extreme resistance — the same bonding technology used in marine construction.

Crystalcare Finish

Anti-bacterial technology built into every finish. Protects both the wood surface and the living environment.
