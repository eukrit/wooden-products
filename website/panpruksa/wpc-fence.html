Premium Co-Extrusion Fence Board

Shield-wrapped composite technology

Our premium co-extrusion fence boards feature an advanced polymer shield layer bonded over a high-density WPC core. This dual-layer construction delivers superior UV resistance, scratch resistance, and fade protection that outperforms traditional composites.

Available in brushed, embossed, and knife-cut textures. Best suited for hotels, resorts, high-end residential estates, and commercial projects.
