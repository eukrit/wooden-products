About us

MAXIS PRODUCTS CO., LTD. (BANGKOK)

MAXISWOOD is a company based in Thailand dedicated to making beautiful building facade using composite timber. Our products look like real wood without cutting down a tree. We use the wastage saw dust of natural types of wood from furniture factories and the timber industry. Our focus is on being environmentally friendly and saving trees. It looks like real wood, but is easier to install and maintain. Our product matches the highest aesthetic and safety standards.

SUSTAINABLE
WOOD EXPERT

MAXISWOOD combines natural
materials from recycled wood to offer an affordable and stunning addition to architecture. We supply a sustainable timber alternative from 100% eco-friendly composite timber material. Our products are also designed with the aim of reducing hi-skill labour needs by being easy and fast to install.
