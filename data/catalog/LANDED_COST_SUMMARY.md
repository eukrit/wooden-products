## Key takeaways

1. **Cheapest per metre (FCL):** Heritage 140×20 decking (LKH-DK-H-140-20) at **THB 62/m**, Heritage cladding (LKH-CL-F-148-21) at **THB 50/m**, Shield cladding grille (LKA-CL-G-121-12) at **THB 46/m**.
2. **Best value Shield panel:** LKA-PN-170-14 at **THB 66/m** — thinnest profile, highest pieces-per-container.
3. **Premium decking ceiling:** LKP-DK-S-200-25 solid at **THB 156/m** — heaviest solid Co-Ex board.
4. **Columns scale hard with size:** 100×100 @ THB 227/m → 200×200 @ THB 488/m. Solid WPC columns beyond 150mm need careful container math.
5. **DIY tiles** economics: mixed pallet of any tile hits a 40HC at ~12k pcs; landed tile cost THB 13–23 each (THB 144–258 per m²).
6. **Fence kit (161.5×20)** lands at THB 92/m — consistent with the 0.3 fence-kit build where 64.46 m of fence totalled THB 285,852 (~4,433/m inclusive of posts, clamps, pedestals, gates).
